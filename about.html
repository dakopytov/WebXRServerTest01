About WebXR

WebXR refers to the technology that enables the development of immersive and interactive experiences accessible through web browsers. It stands for Web Extended Reality and encompasses virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and mixed reality (MR). With WebXR, developers can create applications that leverage the power of XR technology without requiring users to install dedicated apps or software. This opens up new possibilities for creating engaging and accessible XR experiences on the web.
